Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 March 2026

Atavia ("we", "us", or "our") provides a training companion for strength-focused athletes. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Data we collect

  • Account details such as name, email address, and subscription status.
  • Training inputs including readiness check-ins, session selections, and exercise logs.
  • Performance notes such as form feedback, progression notes, and stop-rule outcomes.
  • Device information including app version, device type, and operating system for support.

How we use your data

  • Personalize your training plan, volume adjustments, and session guidance.
  • Track progress, ensure readiness-based recovery, and surface performance insights.
  • Provide customer support and respond to feedback.
  • Maintain app security and improve stability.

Where your data is stored

Training logs and readiness check-ins are stored securely and used to power your program experience. If you use the app offline, some data may be stored locally on your device until it syncs.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers that help us operate the app (such as analytics or hosting), and only to the extent needed to provide the service.

Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Your choices

  • Request access or deletion of your account data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

Contact us

If you have questions, email support@dravora.com.
