Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete all my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, simply uninstall the ATAVIA app from your device. Reinstalling the app will start you with a fresh, empty database. Since we don't store any data externally, uninstalling completely removes all your information.

Can I transfer my data to a new device?

Currently, ATAVIA stores data locally on your device and does not include cloud sync or data export features. If you switch devices, you will need to start fresh. We may add data export functionality in a future update.

What does my readiness status mean?

Your readiness status is calculated based on your daily check-in responses:

  • Ready: Train at full volume with planned progressions.
  • Volume Reduced: Train but reduce total volume by the percentage shown.
  • No Progression: Complete the session but don't increase weights.
  • Rest Day: Skip training and focus on recovery.

Why can't I go back during a training session?

The back button is disabled during exercise execution for safety reasons. This prevents accidental navigation that could cause you to lose your session data or disrupt your training flow. Complete your session or use the provided controls to finish early if needed.

How do I change my training programme?

You can select a different programme from the Programme Selection screen. Your historical data from previous programmes is preserved and can be viewed in Session History.

What are stop rules?

Stop rules are safety guidelines that indicate when you should end a set or exercise. Common stop rules include sharp pain, technique breakdown, dizziness, nausea, or loss of control. Following stop rules helps prevent injury and ensures quality training.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers. Your data is protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal information.

The app crashed or isn't working properly. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure your device has the latest operating system updates
  • Ensure you have the latest version of ATAVIA from the App Store
If the problem persists, please contact us at support@dravora.com with details about what happened, your device model, and iOS version.

Can I request a feature or improvement?

We'd love to hear from you. Send your feature requests and suggestions to support@dravora.com. While we can't implement every request, we review all feedback and use it to guide future development.

Is ATAVIA available on Android?

ATAVIA is currently available for iOS devices. Android availability may be considered in the future based on demand.

Report a Problem

If you encounter a bug, error, or unexpected behaviour in the app, please email us at support@dravora.com with:

  • A description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (if possible)
  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 14 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (e.g., iOS 17.2)
  • The ATAVIA app version (found in the App Store)
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if applicable)

This information helps us identify and fix issues more quickly.
